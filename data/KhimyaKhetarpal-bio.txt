Khimya Khetarpal is a Ph.D. candidate advised by Prof. Doina Precup in
the Reasoning and Learning Lab at McGill University and Mila, Montreal.
Khimya holds an Masters in Computer Engineering from the University of Florida.
She recently worked as a research intern in DeepMind Montreal.
Prior to doctorate studies, Khimya worked at the intersection of computer vision
and robotics at Intel, IIT Kanpur, and Robert Bosch. Her research focuses on developing
RL agents that learn generalized temporal abstractions across both action and perception
for lifelong learning. She is also a robotics enthusiast and enjoys spending time mentoring
younger students outside of research in initiatives such as AI4Good Lab, Skype A Scientist,
and FIRST Robotics.
