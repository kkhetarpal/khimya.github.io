Khimya Khetarpal

I am a Ph.D. candidate with Doina Precup in the Reasoning and Learning Lab at McGill University and Mila, Montreal.

Prior to this, I was working in the Perceptual Computing space on Object Recognition at Intel Corporation, with application to self-driving cars. I completed my Masters in the Electrical & Computer Engineering department at the University of Florida, Gainesville. At UF, I was fortunate to work under the supervision of Prof. Eakta Jain, on modeling saliency in comic art. My research interests span reinforcement learning, computer vision, and robotics.

Research

My work focuses on learning generalized temporal abstractions across both action and perception in the context of lifelong learning. Just like humans acquire skills and build on top of already existing skills to solve more complicated tasks, I am to build AI agents that learn and develop skills continually, hierarchically and incrementally over time.
