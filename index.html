Khimya Khetarpal

I am a Ph.D. candidate with Prof. Doina Precup in the Reasoning and Learning Lab at McGill University and Mila, Montreal.

My research focuses on learning generalized temporal abstractions across both action and perception grounded in theoretical foundations of reinforcement learning. Just like humans acquire skills and build on top of already existing skills to solve more complicated tasks, I aim to build AI agents that learn and develop skills continually, hierarchically and incrementally over time. Broadly, my research interests span reinforcement learning, computer vision, and robotics.
